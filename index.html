Project 1: Enhancing Seller Competitiveness

Description: Conducting an in-depth market analysis to determine key trends and strategies used by competitors. Based on the data obtained, an action plan will be developed for sellers, including recommendations on pricing, assortment, and marketing activities.

Implementation Steps:

  • Market research: Analyzing current market trends, studying consumer preferences, and evaluating competitor activities.
  • Competitor analysis: Identifying the strengths and weaknesses of competitors, studying their promotion strategies and pricing policies.
  • Developing recommendations: Creating an action plan for sellers, including recommendations for improving assortment, pricing, and marketing activities.

Expected Results:

  • Improving seller competitiveness.
  • Increasing sales through effective strategies.
  • Meeting customer needs and increasing their loyalty.

Project 2: Smart Assortment Management

Project Goal: Optimize the assortment of goods for sellers to increase sales and meet customer needs.

Description: Conducting a detailed analysis of the product assortment of sellers, identifying the most and least popular items, and developing recommendations for optimizing the product offering.

Implementation Steps:

  • Assortment analysis: Evaluating the current assortment of goods, identifying the best-selling and least successful items.
  • Researching needs: Analyzing the preferences and needs of customers on the platform.
  • Developing recommendations: Creating recommendations for optimizing the assortment, including introducing new product categories and removing low-selling items.
  • Implementing changes: Assisting sellers in implementing the proposed changes in their assortment.
  • Evaluating results: Regularly analyzing sales and customer satisfaction, adjusting the assortment based on the data obtained.

Expected Results:

  • Increasing sales through assortment optimization.
  • Improving customer satisfaction by better meeting their needs.
  • Reducing storage and promotion costs.
